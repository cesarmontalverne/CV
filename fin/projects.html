Hoosville

2021-2023
  • Planned and developed IOS and Android App with the UVA Psychology department.
  • The research aims at gaining insight about how income and propensity to move influence people's friend-making strategy.
  • The app tries to mimic the world. You need to befriend people while events happen to you.
  • Personal information is gathered through Qualtrics. Game metadata is collected through firebase and converted into dataframes with R.
  • Almost every aspect of the game can be controlled by altering a google sheet.
  • The game is programmed with redux architecture.

USA Fast Food and Obesity

2023
  • Predicted obesity rates on american counties using multiple factors, including fast food restaurants.
  • Used a two step process: clustering data points in all aspects except obesity rates, then a Random Forest algorithm in each region to predict the target.
  • Created plot showing geographically how each cluster was distributed across the country.
  • Used the importance metric from the Random Forests algortithm to establish a correlation between number of fast food restaurants in a county and its obesity rate.
  • See Project!
